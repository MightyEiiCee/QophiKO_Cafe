About Us

Our Company

Qophi Ko aspires to be a world-class coffee shop that caters to local professionals, students from world-class and local universities, and retired individuals who appreciate a friendly, welcoming environment. The main ingredient where everything was all started was from a coffee bean. This coffee bean is from a coffee plant where it was toasted and brewed into the finest coffee powder where we locally transported from from the most sophisticated farm from Cagayan, Philippines where they harvest the quality coffee beans we use for our main products.



Qophi Ko takes pride in pouring a delicious cup of coffee with a side of pastries while providing exceptional customer service with their simple yet sophisticated offerings.



The coffee shop offers variety of choices including coffee, pastries and kinds of refreshing drinks. The choices of products will range from Cold drinks to latte, from lemonade to Ade. The pastries will range from toasts to cakes.

As they serve refreshments to customers of all budgets and backgrounds, their business model is easily adaptable to most locations and partnerships.

Mission and Vision

MISSION

To provide the world's best freshly roasted coffees to our customers while providing excellent service at a price that is fair to both the producers and our customers. The Qophi Ko is defined by our dedication to best practices in customer service, employee well-being, the environment, and community involvement.

VISION

We strive to create a welcoming environment for coffee enthusiasts to escape the hustle and bustle of each day.

Our Partners

We like to say that we are not in the coffee business serving people, but in the people business serving coffee. The heart of the Qophi Ko experience is our partners, our staff. We are dedicated to building a culture of belonging where everyone is accepted, making our partners proud, and investing in their success.

Our People

Our goal is to be a people-positive company that invests in humanity and the well-being of everyone we work with, from our partners to coffee farmers to customers in our stores and beyond.
